ColaCao

by Antonio Garrido

Description

Sometimes there's no time for a proper meal. You may not have the energy to start cooking. A good and trusty ColaCao can save your day. You'll become as strong as the kid above.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Colacao

Steps

There are two main ways:

  • First milk, then ColaCao.
    1. Add milk
    2. Add ColaCao
    3. Stir
  • First ColaCao, then milk.
    1. Add ColaCao
    2. Add a little bit of milk and stir
    3. As you stir, add the rest of the milk
